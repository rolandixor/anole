Place your mouse over any ".an-reveal" to see its corresponding action. Anole reveal animations can be triggered by a simple hover (or tap on mobile), or you can trigger them programatically by adding the "active" class via Javascript. Since these classes use transitions to produce their animations, you can easily adjust their timing by overriding the corresponding
"an-wipe-*"
or
"an-show-*"
classes with your own.

.an-tips
Easily add a tip to element using
.an-tip
.
.an-tip-parent
is required on the element that contains the tip. You can also use
.an-text-tip
if your tip is attached to a block (or span) of text.

See the example below:
<div class="an-text-tip an-tip-parent">Hey kid, I've got a tip: hover the underlined text
    <div class="an-tip-parent">I'm a tip, homie.</div>
</div>
