anole docs - spacers
.an-gen-spacer family
These classes just do what they say on the tin.
They aren't meant to contain anything, and are set to:

`visibility: hidden;`.

Available spacers include:
  • an-gen-spacer-tiny
    - 5px
  • an-gen-spacer-small
    - 15px
  • an-gen-reduced
    - 25px
  • an-gen-spacer
    - 35px
  • an-gen-spacer-large
    - 75px
  • an-gen-spacer-huge
    - 135px
